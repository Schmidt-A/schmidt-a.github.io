About

A flavourful medley of passions, whimsies and hobbies.

My development origin story begins like it does for many: a childhood love of video games. This has since grown into a zest for Python, web development, facilitating life with slick scripts, and a budding interest in UX design.
If you told teenaged me about how excited adult me would be by adding ten pounds of weight plates to a barbell, I'd have never believed you. My body is its own project and weightlifting sessions are the iterations. While I change things up from month to month, I aim to get a barbell into my hands 3-4 times per week. You can see what I'm currently working on here.
With exceptions for occasions and staying social, I'm logging all my macros and eating lots of greens. Health and fitness-seeking requires commitment. That being said, I'm a foodie through-and-through and revel in trying both the best restaurant spots in the city and whatever killer new recipe tickles my fancy. Lots of time is spent in the temple that is my kitchen, be it meal prepping a healthy soup for the week or spoiling myself with homemade peanut butter cups (recipe here, for the curious. Worth every calorie!)
Every journey spent travelling is like an appetizer: quick, delicious, and leaves me hungry for more. Sometimes this takes me as far as Europe, other times it's just a couple hours east at my family's cabin. Happiness is a kayak on a hot day followed by a glass of bubbly on the dock. Maybe Quebec, Japan, Hawaii - I'm not sure what's next, but wherever it is will be an adventure.
Long walks with audiobooks, yoga sessions to counterbalance weight training, art classes, wine education sessions, League of Legends games with friends - the list goes on. Everything as a whole is a spicy recipe that makes for good sampling one day at a time.
